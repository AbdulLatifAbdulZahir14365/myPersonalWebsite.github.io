Welcome to Fredericksburg, VA

I live in the state of Virginia, the city of Fredericksburg

Historical Attractions

Fredericksburg is rich in Civil War history, with sites such as the Fredericksburg & Spotsylvania National Military Park, Mary Washington house, Chatham Manor, and Much more.

Outdoor Activities

The Rappahannock River offers opportunities for fishing, boating, and kayaking. The city also has beautiful parks and trails like Alum Spring park, Motts Run Reservoir Recreation, and Much more.
